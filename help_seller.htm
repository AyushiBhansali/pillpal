Frequently asked questions

What is PillPal?

PillPal is an innovative healthcare platform designed to connect certified sellers with buyers in the healthcare industry. It serves as a secure and accessible marketplace for transparent and trustworthy transactions.

How do I use PillPal as a buyer?

To use PillPal as a buyer, simply create an account, explore the platform, and connect with certified sellers. Browse a diverse range of healthcare products and make informed decisions on your purchases.

How can I join PillPal as a certified seller?

Joining PillPal as a certified seller is a straightforward process. Visit our Seller Registration page, submit the required documentation for certification, and once approved, you can start connecting with buyers and expanding your reach.

Are all sellers on PillPal certified?

Yes, PillPal ensures that all sellers on the platform are certified and meet stringent standards. We prioritize the authenticity and quality of healthcare products to provide a reliable marketplace for our users.
